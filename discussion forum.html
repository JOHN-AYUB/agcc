Community Guidelines

Our forum thrives on mutual respect, helpful contributions, and constructive dialogue. Here's how we maintain a positive environment for everyone.

Community Do's

  • Be respectful and supportive to all members
  • Share practical experiences and proven solutions
  • Use clear, descriptive titles for your posts
  • Include photos when helpful for context
  • Credit sources and acknowledge help received
  • Search before posting to avoid duplicates

Community Don'ts

  • Post spam, advertising, or promotional content
  • Share unverified medical advice for animals
  • Use offensive language or personal attacks
  • Share copyrighted content without permission
  • Post off-topic or irrelevant discussions
  • Share personal contact information publicly
